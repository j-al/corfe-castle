Visitor Information

The castle is easily accessible and is suitable for children.

To get here, you can take the A351 road via Wareham. Or on foot, follow the Purbeck Way from Wareham or Swanage; the nearest train station is Wareham. There are two buses available: the #40 from Poole to Swanage, and the #30 from Swanage to Weymouth.

Address

The Square, Corfe Castle, Nr Wareham, Dorset, BH20 5EZ

There is parking available for both cars and bicycles.
